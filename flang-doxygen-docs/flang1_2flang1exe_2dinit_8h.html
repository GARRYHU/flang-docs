◆ dinit()

void dinit ( VAR *  ivl,
ACL *  ict 
)

Instead of creating dinit records during the processing of data initializations, we need to save information so the records are written at the end of semantic analysis (during semfin). This is necessary for at least a couple of reasons: 1). a record dcl with inits in its STRUCTURE could occur before resolution of its storage class (problematic is SC_CMBLK) 2). with VMS ftn, an array may be initialized (not by implied DO) before resolution of its stype (i.e., its DIMENSION).

The information we need to save is the pointers to the var list and constant tree. This also implies that the getitem areas (4, 5) need to stay around until dinit output.
